My College Life here in University of San Carlos was very challenging, yet harmonious. Being a Carolinian for me is a Gift and pleasure. I can say that I am very lucky to have a School like this, in reality my family can’t send me here in USC due to some financial problems, but because of Passerelles Numeriques NGO I was able to enter here in very prestigious University and it is one of the best Schools here in the Philippines. Being a Scholar of PN was a stepping stone for me. I will climb some steep mountains, run a mile ahead, but it was a key for me to defeat all the barriers that serve as the challenges and difficulties for me to taste the sweet fruit of success. Success that would serve as my accomplishment in life to withstand all of those struggles. And a Carolinian was my best asset in life and I would treasure it my whole life, because I am very lucky enough to have School like this.
